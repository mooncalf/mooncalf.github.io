SERVICES

While I build my portfolio and gain experience, I am offering light copy editing services for free. "Light" copy editing would include correcting obvious errors in spelling, grammar, punctuation, and syntax.

If you would just like to have your work beta read, I am also offering a number of beta reader services (with very light copy editing) for free. If you are interested in having me read your early work, please see Beta Reading below.

This is an opportunity for you to have your work read by someone willing to take more time than the average beta reader, and whose professional focus is specifically on copy editing.

I’ll be using Microsoft Word and the “Track Changes” feature, so I’d appreciate documents be sent to me in Word format, if possible.
